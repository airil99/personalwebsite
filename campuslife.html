Campus Life - My Vacation to Kota Kinabalu

During my recent break, I had the chance to explore the beautiful city of Kota Kinabalu. It was an unforgettable experience, filled with scenic adventures and peaceful moments in nature. I visited the picturesque islands of Manukan and Mamutik, where the crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs offered a perfect escape. Snorkeling here was an absolute highlight, allowing me to see the underwater world up close.

Another memorable part of my journey was visiting the Dairy Farm in Kundasang. Nestled amidst lush green hills with the majestic Mount Kinabalu as a backdrop, the farm provided a refreshing change from the usual city sights. The fresh dairy products were a treat, and the atmosphere was reminiscent of a small countryside in New Zealand. To round off the trip, I explored the lively Kota Kinabalu city, experiencing the local markets, savoring the delicious street food, and enjoying the vibrant energy of the place.
